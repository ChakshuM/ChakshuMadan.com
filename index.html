Professional Profile

Turning Data into
Strategic Decisions

I am a Business Intelligence, Data, and Risk Analytics professional with 11+ years of experience across Banking, Financial Services, and enterprise data environments. Based in the Greater Toronto Area, I specialize in bridging business requirements with data architecture to deliver scalable analytics, executive dashboards, and data-driven decision support.

My core expertise includes SQL, Python, Power BI (DAX and Power Query), ETL pipeline development, and Microsoft Fabric, with hands-on experience supporting regulatory reporting, operational risk management, capital and liquidity analytics, and process automation. I have worked extensively with large, complex datasets to build reliable reporting frameworks and improve data quality and governance.

I hold Microsoft certifications including PL-300 (Power BI Data Analyst Associate) and DP-900 (Azure Data Fundamentals) and am actively advancing into cloud-based data engineering using Azure, Databricks, and modern analytics platforms. I also hold an Ontario Graduate Certificate in Business Insights & Analytics from Humber College.
